Campaign Strategy Pillars

This section details the campaign's strategic foundation, built on four key pillars designed to work in synergy. This integrated approach ensures we engage customers across multiple touchpoints. Use the tabs below to explore each pillar, from interactive digital experiences to high-impact seasonal partnerships.

The 'Pizza Perfect Match' Swipe Game

A central, interactive swipe game within the Domino's app will allow users to 'swipe left' (dislike) or 'swipe right' (like) on various pizza ingredients, styles, and combinations. Each swipe builds a deeper understanding of the player's preferences, culminating in a highly personalised custom pizza recommendation – their "perfect match." If the custom suggestion isn't quite right, the game will then offer alternative pizzas from Domino's existing product range, tailored to the player's expressed likes and dislikes. This gamified approach offers a dynamic and intuitive way to discover personalised pizza choices.

  • Engaging swipe interface for intuitive preference selection.
  • Learns player preferences over time to refine custom pizza suggestions.
  • Suggests a custom "perfect match" pizza based on swipe data.
  • Offers existing Domino's products if custom pizza is not desired.
  • Drives collection of valuable first-party data on customer preferences.

Social Media & User-Generated Content

We will leverage Domino's strong presence on TikTok, Instagram, and X with platform-specific content that encourages participation and sharing. The focus is on humour, relatability, and amplifying authentic user content.

  • TikTok: Branded audio and food challenges (e.g., #MyPizzaMatch).
  • Instagram: Visual UGC contests, interactive Stories, and Reels.
  • X (formerly Twitter): Real-time engagement with trending topics and humorous commentary.
  • UGC Amplification: Promote hashtags and run giveaways to incentivise content creation.

Seasonal Integration & Partnerships

The campaign will tap into major cultural moments to maximise relevance and reach. We will become part of the conversation, not just an advertiser.

  • Love Island (Summer): TV idents using the ad concept, real-time social commentary on villa drama, and a "Love Island Pizza Challenge" to engage the show's highly active audience.
  • Valentine's Day (February): A dual approach with "Perfect Pair" meal deals for couples and "Self-Love" comfort packs for the singles/anti-Valentine's market, ensuring broad appeal.

Promotional Offers & Loyalty Integration

Tailored promotions will incentivise trial and drive sales, while integration with the Domino's Rewards programme will encourage repeat purchases and build long-term loyalty.

  • "Perfect Match" Meal Deals: Tiered bundles for individuals, couples, and groups.
  • Exclusive Discounts: Special offers for orders placed via the Pizza Perfect Match Swipe Game.
  • Loyalty Perks: Double loyalty points on campaign-related orders.
  • Gamified Offers: Small, unexpected upsell prompts at checkout like "love at first bite."

Phased Campaign Rollout

This visual timeline illustrates the campaign's execution plan. A phased approach ensures we build momentum, maximise impact during peak seasons, and sustain engagement over time. Each phase has a distinct focus, allowing for agile adjustments based on performance data.

Phase 1: Pre-Launch Buzz

Building Anticipation

The initial phase focuses on introducing the core campaign concept. We'll launch the "She Doesn't Understand Me Like You Do" ad on TV and digital platforms, creating initial awareness. The Pizza Perfect Match Swipe Game will be soft-launched to gather early user data and feedback.

Phase 2: Peak Activation

Intensified Engagement

During the Love Island season and Valentine's week, we'll amplify all activities. This includes real-time social media activations, deploying seasonal offers, and heavily promoting the swipe game to drive both engagement and sales during these high-relevance periods.

Phase 3: Sustained Momentum

Evergreen Engagement

Post-season, the campaign transitions to an evergreen state. The Pizza Perfect Match Swipe Game becomes a permanent feature on the app and website, continuously providing personalised recommendations. Data collected is used for retargeting and enhancing the loyalty programme.
