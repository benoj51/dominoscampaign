Campaign Strategy Pillars

This section details the campaign's strategic foundation, built on four key pillars designed to work in synergy. This integrated approach ensures we engage customers across multiple touchpoints. Use the tabs below to explore each pillar, from interactive digital experiences to high-impact seasonal partnerships.

The 'Perfect Match' Swipe Game (In-App)

A central, interactive swipe game within the QSR's mobile app allows users to 'swipe left' (dislike) or 'swipe right' (like) on various meal components, sides, and deals. The goal is to shift focus from product to finding the **'perfect deal'** or **'perfect occasion meal'** for the user. This gamified approach drives valuable first-party data collection used by the CRM team.

  • Engaging swipe interface for intuitive preference selection.
  • Learns player preferences to suggest custom meal combinations or bundled deals.
  • Data feeds into CRM (The Platform) to drive cross-selling campaigns (e.g., sides, desserts).
  • Data used for fun, personalised in-app messages (e.g., "Our most unliked topping is X!").

Social Media & User-Generated Content

We will leverage the QSR's strong social presence with platform-specific content that encourages participation and sharing. The focus is on humour, relatability, and amplifying authentic user content.

  • **TikTok:** Branded audio and food challenges (e.g., #MyPerfectMatchDeal).
  • **Instagram:** Visual UGC contests and interactive Reels.
  • **X (formerly Twitter):** Real-time engagement with trending topics and humorous commentary.
  • **UGC Amplification:** Promote hashtags and run giveaways to incentivise content creation.

Seasonal Integration & Partnerships

The campaign will tap into major cultural moments to maximise relevance and reach, aligning with the "servicing occasions" goal.

  • **Major Summer TV Event (e.g., Love Island):** TV idents using the ad concept, real-time social commentary on show drama, and thematic challenges to engage the active audience.
  • **Seasonal Romance Day (e.g., Valentine's Day):** A dual approach with "Perfect Pair" meal deals for couples and "Self-Love" comfort packs for the singles market.

Promotional Offers & Loyalty Integration

Tailored promotions will incentivise trial and drive sales, while integration with the Loyalty Programme will encourage repeat purchases and build long-term retention, particularly focusing on **Average Order Value (AOV)**.

  • **"Perfect Match" Meal Deals:** Tiered bundles focused on adding high-margin items (sides/desserts).
  • **Exclusive Discounts:** Offers for orders placed via the Swipe Game to encourage adoption.
  • **Loyalty Perks:** Double points for campaign-related orders; gamification integrated by Beth's team into the Loyalty Programme.
  • **Gamified Offers:** Upsell prompts designed to increase side purchases at checkout.

Phased Campaign Rollout

This visual timeline illustrates the campaign's execution plan. A phased approach ensures we build momentum, maximise impact during peak seasons, and sustain engagement over time. Each phase has a distinct focus, allowing for agile adjustments based on performance data.

Phase 1: Pre-Launch Buzz

Building Anticipation

The initial phase focuses on introducing the core campaign concept. We'll launch the TV ad on digital platforms. The 'Perfect Match' Swipe Game will be soft-launched to gather early user data and feedback, primarily focusing on data acquisition (for CRM/The Platform use) and CRO.

Phase 2: Peak Activation

Intensified Engagement

During the major TV event season and the Seasonal Romance Day week, we'll amplify all activities. This includes real-time social media activations, deploying seasonal cross-sell offers, and heavily promoting the swipe game to drive both engagement and high AOV sales during these high-relevance periods.

Phase 3: Sustained Momentum

Evergreen Engagement

Post-season, the campaign transitions to an evergreen state. The Swipe Game becomes a permanent feature on the app, continuously providing personalised deal recommendations. Data collected is used for segmenting, retargeting, and enhancing the Loyalty Programme.
